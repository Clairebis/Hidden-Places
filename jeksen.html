JEKSEN DALEN

A free flowing stream, remains of the old mill and a beautiful old stone bridge await.

Explore the surrounding forest with its many small paths and walkways.

DESTINATION OVERVIEW

In 2021 a nature restoration project costing 0.8 million DKK was carried out at Kollens Mølle by the Norwegian Nature Agency.

An old fish ladder and dam were removed, allowing the water to resume its naturalcourse through the valley.

Facilities
