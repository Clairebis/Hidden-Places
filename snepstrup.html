SNEPSTRUP PRÆSTEGÅRD

Discover unusual wooden sculptures, information boards and a 100m long wooden walkway.

Experience nature while learning more about the old Rectory.

DESTINATION OVERVIEW

The Sneptrup Rectory from 1747, with its thatched roof and timbering still stands at the location.

Priest Nicolai Langballe oversaw the building of the Rectory.

Facilities

  • Parking at the old Rectory
  • Covered lunch area
  • Shelters for camping
  • Information boards
  • Toilet
